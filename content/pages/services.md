# Our Services

Rubato Sound Works offers a comprehensive range of professional audio production services. Whatever your project needs, we have the expertise and equipment to deliver exceptional results.

---

## 🎚️ Mixing and Mastering

Transform your raw recordings into polished, radio-ready tracks. Our mixing and mastering services ensure your music sounds professional across all playback systems.

**What We Offer:**
- Multi-track mixing with professional plugins
- Stereo and stem mastering
- Loudness optimization for streaming platforms
- Unlimited revisions until you're satisfied
- Reference track matching
- Delivery in multiple formats (WAV, MP3, etc.)

---

## 🎤 Recording Studio

Our acoustically treated recording studio is equipped with industry-standard microphones, preamps, and monitoring systems to capture your best performance.

**Facilities Include:**
- Vocal booth with premium microphones
- Live room for instruments
- High-end preamps and converters
- Professional monitoring system
- Comfortable and inspiring environment
- Session musicians available on request

---

## 🎬 Dubbing

High-quality dubbing services for films, web series, advertisements, and multimedia content. Our experienced voice artists and engineers ensure perfect sync and natural delivery.

**Services Include:**
- Voice-over recording
- ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement)
- Multiple language support
- Lip-sync dubbing
- Character voice work
- Clean, broadcast-quality audio

---

## 🔊 SFX (Sound Effects)

Custom sound effects design and production for films, games, advertisements, and multimedia projects. From Foley to digital sound design, we create immersive audio experiences.

**We Provide:**
- Custom Foley recording
- Digital sound design
- Audio restoration and cleanup
- Ambience and atmosphere creation
- Sound effects library access
- 5.1/7.1 surround sound mixing

---

## 🎼 Short Film Scoring

Original music composition and scoring tailored to your film's emotional narrative. We work closely with directors to create the perfect sonic landscape for your story.

**Our Process:**
- Collaborative spotting sessions
- Custom composition and orchestration
- Live instrument recording (if needed)
- Mixing to picture
- Stems delivery for final mix
- Multiple revision rounds

---

Full-service song recording from pre-production to final mix. Whether you're a solo artist or a band, we help you realize your musical vision.

**What's Included:**

- Mixing and basic mastering

## 🎹 Music Production

Complete music production services from concept to finished track. We handle arrangement, programming, recording, mixing, and mastering.

Professional acoustic treatment solutions to optimize your recording space or listening environment. We design and implement treatments tailored to your room's unique acoustics.
