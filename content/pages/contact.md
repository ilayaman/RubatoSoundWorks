To help us understand your project better and provide accurate information, please include:

1. **Your Name & Contact Details**
2. **Type of Service Needed** (Mixing, Recording, Scoring, etc.)
3. **Project Details** (genre, duration, scope)
4. **Timeline** (when do you need it completed?)
5. **Budget Range** (helps us tailor our proposal)
6. **Reference Links** (if applicable)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you offer free consultations?**  
Yes! We're happy to discuss your project and provide guidance at no cost.

**Can I visit the studio before booking?**  
Absolutely! We encourage studio visits. Just schedule an appointment first.

**What payment methods do you accept?**  
We accept bank transfer, UPI, cash, and all major digital payment methods.

**Do you require advance payment?**  
We typically require 50% advance for new clients, with the balance due upon delivery.

**What's your cancellation policy?**  
Cancellations made 24 hours before the scheduled session receive a full refund of the advance. Later cancellations may incur charges.
